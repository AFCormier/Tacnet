    About:

    Tacnet was created by two computer science students with the intention of making it easier to plan tactics ahead of matches in different competitive games. If you want to contribute or just check out the code, feel free to fork our repo over at GitHub.

    Donations:

    Unfortunately, servers aren't free, and neither are domains. Even though we've received feedback from users that would pay for Tacnet, one of the main points has always been to keep it free and without annoying ads, which has worked out quite well so far. If you do wish to support us though, you can do so through PayPal.
